About us

The Online Art Academy is an online school that is taught by highly skilled and experienced professionals. All these professionals have either majored in art at a 4 year college or have spent years at a college specifically dedicated to teaching art These professionals can teach all ages and many different skills. These professionals have taught art at other schools before working here. They either taught college art or high school art . They have all taught in most areas of art and not just one area. They all teach multiple classes with all different skill levels and ages.
